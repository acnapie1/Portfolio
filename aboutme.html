About me

Hi. I am Abby Napier. I am a Graphic Information Technology major at Arizona State University. I am a very creative person. I can fit and conform my designs to any crowd. I have traveled to various countries and experienced many different cultures. I also enjoy animals, agriculture, nature, and various art styles. I ride horses, I have a farm, I enjoy cooking, and doing art. This gives me a unique twist on all of my work. I can design your website and grow the best carrots you have ever had. I would love to work with a company looking for new and fresh designs. If you would like to talk more please contact me. I look forward to hearing from you.
